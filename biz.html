Course Description

Business Administration is the study of managing organizations to achieve goals efficiently and effectively. This course provides a high-level overview of essential business functions, including strategic planning, financial accounting, human resource management, and marketing. Students will analyze real-world case studies to develop critical thinking skills necessary for leading in a competitive global marketplace.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the core principles of organizational behavior and leadership.
  • Analyze financial statements to assess company health and performance.
  • Develop comprehensive marketing strategies using market research and consumer data.
  • Apply project management methodologies to drive operational efficiency.
  • Understand the legal and ethical frameworks governing corporate environments.
  • Master the basics of Supply Chain Management and logistics.
  • Explore Entrepreneurship and the process of launching a new venture.
  • Apply SWOT analysis and Porter's Five Forces to strategic planning.
  • Demonstrate effective business communication and negotiation skills.
  • Navigate international business challenges and cross-cultural management.
