Course Description

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) focuses on the intersection of technology and business needs. This course covers the design and management of IT infrastructure, including local and wide-area networks (LAN/WAN), cloud services, and cybersecurity essentials. You will gain hands-on experience in system administration, database management, and the deployment of enterprise-level communication tools.

Learning Objectives

  • Configure and troubleshoot enterprise network architectures (TCP/IP).
  • Deploy and manage cloud computing resources (AWS/Azure).
  • Install and maintain server operating systems (Windows Server/Linux).
  • Understand the principles of database administration and SQL.
  • Implement cybersecurity protocols to protect organizational assets.
  • Manage IT projects using Agile and ITIL frameworks.
  • Master web development and digital content management.
  • Configure Voice over IP (VoIP) and digital communication systems.
  • Perform hardware maintenance and technical support troubleshooting.
  • Understand the ethical use of data and ICT governance.
